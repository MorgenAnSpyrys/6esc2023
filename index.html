Overview

6 Digit European Suiji Cup (6ESC) is a 6 Digit (100k+) 4v4 teamsize 8 Suiji National tournament limited to European* countries. Suiji format allows unlimited signups, but only the x highest rank players qualify. For this tournament that means countries will not have tryouts, the players 8 ranked closest to 100k after "quasi-BWS" when regs close will represent their country.

Quasi Badge-Weighting-System (BWS)

This Tournament uses a quasi Badge-Weighting-System (BWS). This is because tournaments at this rank range are not badged, but we want to exclude derankers who ruin the fun for everyone else.

Eligibility

The original quasi-bws functioned by relying on user self-reports, and we have since deprecated it in favour of the following system:
Players ranked between and including 100000 to 999999 Post-BWS at time of registration closing may be eligible to participate.
Ranking to 99999 (post-BWS) or higher within 90 days of regs closing will result in ineligibility. This is meant to discourage deranking.
We will be using BWS where recent tournament wins will be counted as "badges". This may affect your eligibility, so make sure to use the BWS calculator provided on this website.
